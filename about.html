Our Company

Company Overview

Established in 2015, Techvivid Systems (TVSL) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified system integrator with a strong presence across the Middle East, Africa, and East Asia. We deliver converged IT and OT infrastructure that is secure, scalable, and future-ready, empowering businesses to succeed in the digital age.

Our core expertise spans enterprise IT infrastructure, industrial automation, IT/OT cybersecurity, smart surveillance, IoT and edge deployments, telecom system integration, structured cabling, and data center modernization.

Backed by over a decade of cross-industry experience, our multidisciplinary team brings together deep technical expertise, field-proven methodologies, and a strong commitment to service excellence. We support mission-critical environments across manufacturing, utilities, transport, public sector, and smart infrastructure, acting as a strategic partner with 24x7 support to ensure performance, compliance, and resilience.
